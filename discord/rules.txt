# <:Rules:1525846007093657750>Rules
Please make sure to read these rules before continuing to the server. Failure to comply with these rules will result in action being taken. If a rule is not in the list, it does not mean the rule doesn't apply. The moderators will be the final judge on the action, if you believe the moderator chose wrong. Notify a higher-up

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894>Section 1: Chatting
- Everyone is to be resepected. We expect members to treat eachother with respect
- Our main language is Dutch, but English is also allowed. Any other languages are prohibited

### Spamming
Spamming is strictly prohibited, any spamming or intentional disruption will not be tolerated and will immediately be moderated. Spamming includes any kind of rapid messaging, wether this is chats, images, gifs or emojis.

### Inappropriate Content
Conversations about inappropriate content are not allowed within this server. While chatting please use discretion and listen to the moderators if they tell you to switch topics. Inappropriate topics may include, but not exclusively:
- Sex or sexual innuendos
- Drugs/Alcohol
   - In discussions about incidents the emergency services responded to, this is allowed.
- Mild swearing is permitted, but anything excessive is not allowed
- Widely controversial or sensitive topics
- Racism, sexism, transphobia, homophobia, and harassment
- Discussing any moderation actions in public chat; please submit a ticket to discuss further
### Advertising
- Any advertising of your own servers or websites is strictly prohibited. This includes sending external links or soliciting people.
- Posting random links, such as social media or article links, in chat without prior context or reasoning is prohibited

### Harassment
- Any kind of bullying is not allowed in any channel
   - This is classified as negative comments and or mockery to a person with negative intent
- Racism, sexism, transphobia, homophobia, and threatening is strictly prohibited
- Provoking or pressuring another member to break the rules is not allowed
- Any kind of promotion of suicide or self harm is strictly prohibited 

### Impersonation
Any kind of impersonation will not be allowed in any channel. Impersonation includes, but is not exclusive to:
- Pretending to be someone you are not
- Pretending to have a role in a company, which you don't have
- Claiming ownership of work or contributions you did not do

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894>Section 2: Voice Channels
- Do not spam your microphone in any of the voice channels
- Voice channels are expected to be appropriate. Any inappropriate messages or streams will result in immediate action
- Any kind of harassment is strictly prohibited. This includes negative comments or mockery
- Do not use any kind of inappropriate voice changers or sounds. This includes so called "Ear Rape"

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894>Section 3: Miscelanious
### Profile Pictures
Although profile pictures are not directly created within the server, they must follow certain rules to keep safety among the community and respect for others. When choosing a profile picture, the following are prohibited:
- Racism, sexism, transphobia, or homophobia
- Inappropriate content (gore, violence, or NSFW)
- Flashing photo pictures that can trigger epilepsy
### Custom names
Any kind of custom names/nicknames that are deemed inappropriate, will be moderated

### Bypassing
- Typing letters or symbols to bypass the rules will not be tolerated
- Using external emojis inappropriately
-  Automated user accounts/self bots are not permitted under any circumstances
-  Alternate accounts are not allowed to bypass mutes/bans and will result in addition punishment for the main account

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894>Section 4: Moderation
Complaining about your disciplinary action in chat will lead to further punishment. Do not show screenshots of moderation actions in chat, as this disrupts the conversation of chat and is private between you and the Moderation Department. Any commenting will result in additional moderation.

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894>Section 5: Appeals
If you believe the moderators made a wrong decision based on your behaviour. Please create a ticket in <#1525129839550529596> and give your reason for creating your ticket and showing proof of the wrong moderation. This appeal will be reviewed and discussed between the staff. If the action is deemed to be wrong, we will undo it immediately
