# <:Info_Moderator:1525846357854916790> Info
Welcome to the official server of the HulpdienstvoertuigenBeNeLux website. In this server you can receive information about website updates, events, social posts and many other things. Chat with fellow fans, share your thoughts on the website and talk about real life incidents. 

HulpdienstvoertuigenBeNeLux is a website filled with information about the emergency services of the BeNeLux, from vehicles to buildings, everything is able to be found on the website.

Before you continue to the rest of the server, make sure to read the <#1509163707706380478> so that you don't violate any of them. **Please note:** this server is primarily Dutch, but all the information channels will be in English for any non Dutch speakers

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894> Volunteers
To keep the website info as up-to-date as possible, we have a team of volunteers helping us with researching information. We greatly appreciate those people and are always looking to improve the efficiency. 

If you would like to become a volunteer, please create a ticket. Please note that we will not freely handout the volunteer role as it is a very sensitive position. You will have to prove that you are trustworthy before being allowed into the team.

## <:Arrow:1525846058049994894> FAQ
> **Q: Which countries are included on the site?**
> A: The Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg. On the website you can find more information about each.
> 
> **Q: Can I use photos from the website?**
> A: That depends on the licensing and permission of the photographer. Always contact the photographer first. 
> 
> **Q: Can I submit my own photos?**
> A: Yes, visitors can also submit photos, make sure your photos do not have any privacy sensitive information. You can submit photos [here](https://hulpdienstvoertuigenbenelux.nl/upload)
> 
> **Q: Are submitted photos always posted?**
> A: No, not always. The photos will be reviewed based on quality, relevancy and safety.
> 
> **Q: How is the information about the vehicles being collected?**
> A: Through open sources, spotters, official press releases and the emergency services themselves. Along with contributions from visitors
> 
> **Q: Can I report a mistake in information?**
> A: Yes you can, send a form [here](https://hulpdienstvoertuigenbenelux.nl/contact) or make a ticket in <#1457685596465266834> 
> 
> **Q: Can I reach out?**
> A: You can reach out to us [here](https://hulpdienstvoertuigenbenelux.nl/contact) or make a ticket in <#1457685596465266834> 
> 
> **Q: Can I collaborate or become a partner?**
> A: Yes you can. If you are interested, contact one of the admins or make a ticket in <#1457685596465266834>
